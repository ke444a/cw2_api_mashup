Frequently Asked Questions


Q: How do I find the cheapest games?

A: Go onto the Discover Tab on the Home page and use the filter system to refine your searches.

Q: How do I sign up?

A: Vapour is an entirely front-end interface. There are no user profiles and we do not save any of your data.

Q: Why should I use Vapour?

A: Our system does not require you to provide any personal data before showing you the best and most up-to-date deals on the hottest games.

Q: Can I see more information about the games?

A: Yes! Once you find a game on the Discover Tab, hit the More Info button. This will show you everything you need to know about the game.

Q: Why can't I buy games here?

A: Vapour is designed to show you the best and most up-to-date deals on all your favourite games. In order to focus solely on this, we do not offer transactional services, instead directing you to other sites to make your purchase.

Q: How can I let you know of an issue or suggestion?

A: If you need to contact us for whatever reason, head over to our Contact Us page. You can reach us via the Telephone, private message services on our social media or send us an email.

Still curious or unsure? Ask us anything by heading over to our Contact Us page for details on how to get in touch!
